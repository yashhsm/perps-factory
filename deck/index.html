THE PROBLEM

Perps markets are
gated and slow

Listing Bottleneck

New assets wait weeks for governance votes. Memecoins, RWAs, and long-tail assets can't get perps markets. Traders miss opportunities.

Liquidity Cold Start

New markets launch with zero depth. Market makers won't quote until volume exists. Volume won't come without depth. Chicken-and-egg.

Centralized Control

Teams decide which markets exist. Fee parameters are top-down. Creators who identify demand can't capture value from it.

Fragmented Liquidity

Each perps protocol is a silo. Traders choose platforms, not markets. Capital efficiency suffers across the ecosystem.

HOW IT WORKS

AMM-bootstrapped
virtual liquidity

  • Constant-product AMM with virtual reserves
  • No real liquidity needed at launch
  • Pyth oracle for index price
  • Direction penalty widens spread on imbalanced OI
  • 8-hour funding rate keeps mark ~ index
  • Permissionless liquidation with keeper rewards

Mark Price

P = AMM_quote / AMM_base

Virtual reserves determine the mark price. Trades shift the curve. Funding rate anchors it to Pyth index.

Direction Penalty

spread += |OI_long - OI_short| * k

When OI is imbalanced, the AMM charges more. Protects LPs from directional risk.
