HOW IT WORKS

AMM-bootstrapped
virtual liquidity

  • Constant-product AMM with virtual reserves
  • No real liquidity needed at launch
  • Pyth oracle for index price, or deployer-pushed
  • Direction penalty widens spread on OI imbalance
  • 8-hour funding rate keeps mark ~ index
  • Permissionless liquidation with keeper rewards
  • Deployer-configurable OI caps and leverage tiers

Mark Price

P = AMM_quote / AMM_base

Virtual reserves determine the mark price. Trades shift the curve. Funding rate anchors it to Pyth index.

Direction Penalty

spread += |OI_long - OI_short| * k

When OI is imbalanced, the AMM charges more. Protects LPs from directional risk. Inspired by HL's funding multipliers.

Key Insight

HIP-3 markets launch empty and need HIP-2 (Uniswap-style) bootstrapping. We bake the AMM into the perps engine itself — every market has depth from block 1.

BUSINESS MODEL

Three-way
fee split

Protocol
Fixed %

Every trade, every market. Like HL's protocol fee — scales with total volume. Goes to treasury.

Builder
Configurable

Market deployers earn fees on every trade. Like HIP-3's 50% share, but tunable. Incentivizes market creation.

LPs
Spread + Fees

LPs earn from AMM spread. Direction penalty reduces adverse selection. Like HL's HLP but per-market.

Flywheel: Builders create markets (free) -> AMM provides instant depth -> traders arrive -> fees flow to builders + LPs -> more builders deploy
